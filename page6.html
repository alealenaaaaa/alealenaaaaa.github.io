This is Alex, a working adult with a successful business.
One day, he receives a call from someone he trusts —
a friend from way back. He picks up the phone.
On the other end, his friend sounds worried and stressed.
They say:
“Hey Alex! It's Stephan. I'm in a tight situation right now,
can you lend me $1000 please?
I'll pay you back next month! I promise!”
